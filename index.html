Welcome to Astronomical Spectroscopy

Spectroscopy provides a window into the physics of our Universe and allows the study of the motions, composition, and characteristics in astronomical phemomenon. The goals of this course are to explore how spectroscopic data is obtained and analyzed to explore the physical processes of distant objects. This will take us on a spectral tour of the Universe, from which we can measure the tempartures, pressures, densities, dynamics, and magnitic fields of the great diversity of astrophysical sources.
