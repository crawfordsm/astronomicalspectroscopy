Astronomical Spectroscopy

Using Spectroscopy to discover our Universe. A NASSP Honours Course

ArXiv Project

Due: 29 July 2016

Type: Individual

Description: The goal of this project is to summarize a recent paper on astro-ph that is related to spectroscopy. The student should select a paper from Recent astro-ph list that is related to spectroscopy and provide a maximum of a one-page summary of the paper and the underlying spectroscopic principles of the paper.

Submission: Electronic versions of the reports should be submitted as pull requests in the projects/ directory with a format of arxiv_surname.pdf used to name each file.
